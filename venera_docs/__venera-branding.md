# Dr. Venera Frolova - Aesthetic Doctor Website Branding Guide

## Business Overview

- **Name**: Dr. Venera Frolova
- **Location**: Bali, Indonesia
- **Target**: Russian expats + expand to international clients
- **Services**: **Injection-only, non-surgical** aesthetic treatments
  - Mesotherapy, Fillers, Skin Boosters, Botox, Russian Lips
  - Acne/Pigmentation/Rosacea package, Peeling, Facial Cleansing
  - Lipolytics, Polylactic Acid, Men's Treatments

## Key Distinction (Repeat Throughout Site)

**"Certified doctor specializing in NON-SURGICAL injectable treatments only - NOT a plastic surgeon"**

## Professional Title Options (Ranked by Recommendation)

| Title                          | Why It Works                                     | Preserves Russian "Cosmetologist"? |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| **Aesthetic Doctor**           | Medical authority + familiar to English speakers | No                                 |
| **Cosmetic Dermatologist**     | Skin expertise + medical prestige                | Partial ("cosmetic")               |
| **Aesthetic Cosmetologist MD** | Bridges Russian term + clarifies medical         | ✅ Yes                             |
| **Physician Cosmetologist**    | Doctor-first + keeps cosmetology                 | ✅ Yes                             |
| **Dermatocosmetologist**       | Specialized hybrid term                          | ✅ Yes                             |

**Recommendation**: Use **"Aesthetic Cosmetologist MD"** as primary title to honor Russian roots while clarifying medical expertise.
